Long-Term Care Planning: What Is It?

It’s a personalized strategy that sets aside resources for extended care needs and outlines how and where you prefer to receive that care. Your plan can coordinate with existing coverage or stand on its own. Options may support assistance at home, adult day services, or residential care so you can choose what feels right. The result: comfort, continuity, and confidence for you and your loved ones.
